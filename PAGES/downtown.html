Welcome to Downtown

Explore Downtown Pittsburgh, the city's central district where skyscrapers meet the three rivers and culture thrives.

About Downtown

Known as the Golden Triangle, Downtown sits at the confluence of the Allegheny and Monongahela Rivers. It has long been Pittsburgh's commercial heart, filled with historic architecture alongside modern skyscrapers, theaters, and sports venues.

Notable Landmarks

Point State Park, Market Square, and the gleaming towers of PPG Place define Downtown's skyline and celebrate Pittsburgh's rich history.

Unique Characteristics

Downtown blends corporate headquarters with acclaimed theaters and restaurants. The Speckled Egg and Cafe Momentum highlight a culinary renaissance that draws locals and visitors alike.

BMW Connection

Downtown's blend of historic buildings and cutting-edge developments mirrors BMW's commitment to pairing tradition with forward-thinking design.
